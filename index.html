Where is Laamu Maamendhoo???

Laamu Maamendhoo is located southern limit of Central Maldives. which is in Laamu Atoll or also known as Haddhunmathi Atoll. it is 260.84 km (162.08 mi) far away from Maldives Capital city, with a population of 1,303 people (infomation from:https://isles.gov.mv/Island/DetailsEn/818 and wikipedia)
