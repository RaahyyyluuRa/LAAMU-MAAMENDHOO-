Haddhunmathi Laamu Maamendhoo Council Idhaara

Haddhunmathi Laamu Maamendhoo Council idhaara lead and represent their communities. the council engages with their communities and encourage community participation in decision-making, while considering the needs of people currently living in communities and those who will live there in the future.

Some programs/events conducted by The council

  • M.R.P.S Quran competion
  • Hepta Council Ramadan futsal Cup 2023 ( men and women)
